Olivia Sessions

Hello! My name is Olivia. I am from North Carolina and I am a junior studying bioinformatics here at BYU. Although I am new to all the computer science stuff, I am excited to learn some marketable programming skills. I got back from my mission in Washington state a year and a half ago and got married a month ago. I love being a student at BYU and am excited for what the future has in store!
